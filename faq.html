FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you show fares on the website?

We don't publish fares online. Every trip — whether a fixed route or a custom destination — is quoted directly by our team once we know your route, date, vehicle and passenger count. Fill in the booking form and confirm your fare with us on WhatsApp.

What's the difference between a Fixed Route and a Custom Destination booking?

Fixed Routes cover our most-travelled corridors — Nashik ⇄ Pune, Nashik ⇄ Mumbai, Nashik ⇄ Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar, and Mumbai ⇄ Pune — with pickup and drop preset for you. Custom Destination lets you type any pickup and drop, including Shirdi, Trimbakeshwar or anywhere else in India.

How do I confirm my booking?

After submitting the booking form, a prefilled WhatsApp message opens summarising pickup, drop, date, time and vehicle. Send it and our team confirms your driver and car.

Can I book a round trip?

Yes — select "Round Trip" on the booking form and mention your return date in the notes field so we can plan the driver's schedule.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Message us on WhatsApp or call our booking line as early as possible. Cancellations closer to pickup time may be subject to a driver allocation charge.

What payment methods are accepted?

Cash, UPI and card payments to the driver are accepted. Partial advance payment can be arranged over WhatsApp for group and tempo traveller bookings.

Do you provide airport pickups?

Yes, for both Terminal 1 and Terminal 2 at Mumbai Airport. Share your flight number in the notes field and the driver will track it for delays.

Is luggage space guaranteed?

Vehicle capacity listed on the fleet page includes standard luggage. For extra bags or equipment, mention it in the booking notes so we assign a larger vehicle if needed.
