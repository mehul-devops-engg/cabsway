Popular Routes

Fixed routes, plus anywhere you need to go

These four corridors are our most-booked fixed routes, run daily in both directions. Need somewhere else — Shirdi, Trimbakeshwar or another town? Book it as a custom destination and we'll quote you directly on WhatsApp.

About These Routes

Nashik ⇄ Pune

Our busiest corridor, run daily for business travellers, students and families. Sedans, SUVs and tempo travellers are all available for the Nashik–Pune stretch.

Nashik ⇄ Mumbai

City-to-city cabs between Nashik and Mumbai, with drop-offs at Mumbai Airport available on request — just mention it in your booking notes.

Nashik ⇄ Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar

A reliable connect between Nashik and Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar (formerly Aurangabad), popular for business trips and visits to the Ajanta & Ellora Caves.

Mumbai ⇄ Pune

Expressway cabs connecting Maharashtra's two largest cities, available as one-way or round trips.

Custom Destinations

Beyond our fixed routes, CabsWay also runs custom trips to Shirdi (Sai Baba Samadhi Mandir), Trimbakeshwar (one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, 30 minutes from Nashik), and anywhere else in India. Just choose "Custom Destination" on the booking page.
