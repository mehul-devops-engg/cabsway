About Us

Built by people who drive these roads weekly

CabsWay — sometimes searched as Cabs Way or Cab's Way — started as a small fleet running the Nashik–Mumbai highway and grew into a network covering Nashik, Pune, Mumbai and Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar, with custom trips to Shirdi, Trimbakeshwar and beyond.

What we do

We run fixed daily routes between Nashik, Pune, Mumbai and Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar, plus custom trips to Shirdi, Trimbakeshwar or anywhere else in India. Fares aren't published online — every trip is quoted directly by our team once we know your route, date and vehicle.

How we operate

Vehicles, trips and bookings run through a single operations dashboard, so seats are tracked in real time and a car is never booked past its capacity.

Our fleet

Hatchbacks, sedans, SUVs and tempo travellers — each inspected and insured, matched to your group size on the fleet page.

Getting in touch

Every booking is confirmed on WhatsApp with your driver and car details. Reach us any time via the contact page.

Ready to book your route?

Choose a fixed route or a custom destination, and confirm your fare with our team on WhatsApp in under two minutes.
