Frequently Asked Questions

Do you show fares on the website?

No — fares aren't published online. Every trip is quoted directly by our team once we know your route, date and vehicle. Send us your details from the booking page and we'll confirm the fare on WhatsApp.

How do I confirm my booking?

After you submit the booking form, we open a pre-filled WhatsApp message summarising your trip. Send it and our team confirms your fare, car and driver within minutes.

Can I book a custom destination that isn't a fixed route?

Yes. Choose "Custom Destination" on the booking form, pick your starting city — Nashik, Pune, Mumbai or Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar — and type any destination anywhere in India.

What payment methods are accepted?

Cash, UPI, and card payments to the driver are accepted. Advance/partial payment can be arranged over WhatsApp for group bookings.
